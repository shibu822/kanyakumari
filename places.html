Top Attractions in Kanyakumari

Discover the must-see landmarks and attractions in Kanyakumari, the southernmost tip of India.

Island monument dedicated to Swami Vivekananda

Kanyakumari Beach

Famous for its sunrise and sunset views

Padmanabhapuram Palace

Historic wooden palace complex

Thiruvalluvar Statue

133-ft statue of Tamil philosopher

Gandhi Memorial Mandapam

Memorial where Gandhi's ashes were kept before immersion

Kanyakumari Temple

Ancient temple dedicated to Goddess Kanyakumari

Coastal fort offering panoramic sea views
